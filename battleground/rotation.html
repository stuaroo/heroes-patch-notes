Ranked Map Rotation

since 2019-08-06

  • Battleground rotations were introduced in the patch notes from 2017-05-16
  • Originally applying to all game modes, the rotation has since been amended to only include Ranked play
  • The pool consists of 9 Battlegrounds which rotate approximately 3 times per Ranked Season (or about once a month) each Ranked Season
    • Pool was reduced to 8 Battlegrounds on 2017-09-05, the patch following Hanamura's removal from all games modes to undergo changes on 2017-08-08
    • Pool was increased back to 9 Battlegrounds on 2017-09-26, including the newly released Volskaya Foundry
      • Pool was temporarily reduced to 8 Battlegrounds on 2018-06-18 as Volskaya Foundry was removed following reports of a bug involving Medivh's Raven Form; Volskaya returned to the pool on 2018-06-27
  • See the original blog post on Battleground rotations for more information
