Rework Diffs

What is a rework diff?

A diff is a computing term meaning "compare (files) in order to determine how or whether they differ." Instead of files we are comparing Heroes: pre-rework and post-rework. I've highlighted differences (additions in green, subtractions in red) to make them stand out and (hopefully) give a better high-level view of all these changes.
