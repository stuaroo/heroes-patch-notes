2018-01-16 Rework Diffs

Looking for an overview of changes from a recent hero rework? You've found the place. Green stuff is new, red has been removed. Refer to the Legend below for specifics on box and line highlights.

Legend

  • New
    This talent is all shiny and new, and completely green in color
  • Changed
    Updated
    The green highlights indicate the new description
    While the red highlights show what the description used to say
  • Moved
    Moved from level X
    This talent has been moved levels; it will have a blue label describing the move.
    Will generally be followed by a red section listing the old description too
  • Unchanged
    These white boxes are exactly as they were before; nothing changed here
  • Removed
    This removed talent is entirely red and sad because it no longer exists for this hero :(

Tyrael

Stats
  • Base Maximum Health increased from 2296 to 2468
  • Health Regeneration increased from 4.78 to 5.14
  • Passive Spell Armor has been removed

Abilities

  • Q
    El'druin's Might
    Mana: 45 50  Cooldown: 12
    Throw El'druin to the target area, dealing 110 (+4% per level) damage to nearby enemies and Slowing them by 25% for 2.5 seconds. Reactivating will teleport Tyrael to El'druin and Slow nearby enemies again.
  • W
    Righteousness
    Updated
    Mana: 50 60  Cooldown: 10
    Shields Tyrael for 336 (+4% per level) damage and nearby allies for 40% 135 (+4% per level) as much for 4 seconds.
  • E
    Smite
    Updated
    Mana: 45  Cooldown: 6
    Rake target area for 150 144 (+4% per level) damage. Allies moving through the targeted area gain 25% increased Movement Speed for 2 seconds.
    Can now be cast while moving
  • R
    Judgment
    Mana: 80  Cooldown: 70
    After 0.75 seconds, charge an enemy Hero dealing 150 (+4% per level) damage and stunning them for 1.5 seconds. Nearby enemies are knocked away and take 75 (+4% per level) damage.
  • R
    Sanctification
    Mana: 75  Cooldown: 100
    After 0.5 seconds create a field of holy energy that makes allied Heroes Invulnerable. Lasts 3 seconds.
  • Trait
    Archangel's Wrath
    Upon dying, become Invulnerable and explode for 550 (+4% per level) damage after 3.5 seconds.
