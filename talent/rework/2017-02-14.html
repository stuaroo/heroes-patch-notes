2017-02-14 Rework Diffs

Looking for an overview of changes from a recent hero rework? You've found the place. Green stuff is new, red has been removed. Refer to the Legend below for specifics on box and line highlights.

Legend

  • New
    This talent is all shiny and new, and completely green in color
  • Changed
    Updated
    The green highlights indicate the new description
    While the red highlights show what the description used to say
  • Moved
    Moved from level X
    This talent has been moved levels; it will have a blue label describing the move.
    Will generally be followed by a red section listing the old description too
  • Unchanged

Murky

Developer Comments: Mrrgrgrlgrl! Rise my brother murlocs! Mrrrgrgrlrlgrl! Vengeance will be ours! In examining Murky, his original health and egg timers are vestiges of a different time, created before even Jaina entered the Nexus. We wanted to give him more base health to make him a little harder to burst down, but traded it for a little longer time to respawn from his Egg. This has the added bonus of allowing a bit more counter-play to hunting our fishy friend. We also wanted to adjust Pufferfish, as an increasingly high amount of the roster could immediately explode it. Finally, his talent tree has been updated to come in line with our new philosophies. We've not only found this Murky much more fun to play as, with some exciting talents in each tier, but also more fair to fight against! Mrrgrgrlgrl!

Stats
  • Base Maximum Health increased from 635 to 761

Abilities

  • (Q) Slime Updated
     CD: 4s
    Deal 86 (+4% per level) damage and apply Slime on nearby enemies for 8 seconds, slowing them by 25% 20%. Deal 210 205 (+4% per level) damage to enemies who are already Slimed.
  • (W) Pufferfish Updated
     CD: 15s
    Spit out a Pufferfish with 225 (+5.5% per level) health at the target point. After 3 seconds, the fish will blow up for 410 (+4% per level) damage. Deals 50% less damage to Structures.
    Explosion radius increased by 50%
  • (E) Safety Bubble
     CD: 14s
    Becomes Invulnerable for 2 seconds. While active, Murky cannot attack or use abilities.
  • (R) March of the Murlocs Updated
     CD: 80s 100s
    After 0.75 seconds, Murky commands a legion of Murlocs to march in a target direction, each one leaping onto the first enemy Hero or Structure they find. Each Murloc deals 125 (+4% per level) damage and slows its target by 15% for 5 seconds. Murlocs deal 50% damage to Structures.
    Increased the Movement Speed of the tiny murlocs by 30%
  • (R) Octo-Grab
     CD: 50s
    Murky summons an octopus to stun target enemy Hero for 3 seconds while he hits them for 1 damage a second.
  • (D) Spawn Egg Updated
     CD: 15s
    Place an Egg at target location, revealing the nearby area. Upon dying, Murky respawns at the Egg after 8 5 seconds. Murky only grants 25% of a real Hero's experience upon dying. If Murky's Egg is killed, he is revealed to enemies for 15 seconds, and Spawn Egg is placed on cooldown.
