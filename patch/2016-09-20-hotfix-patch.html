Heroes of the Storm Hotfix Patch Notes – September 20, 2016

Official Patch Notes

We’ve just released a new patch for Heroes of the Storm in order to apply a few bug fixes. Check out the patch notes below.

Bug Fixes

Heroes and Talents

  • Sgt. Hammer: Fixed an issue in which each of Sgt. Hammer's Basic Attacks could reduce her Ability cooldowns multiple times after learning the Focused Attacks Talent.

User Interface

  • General: Heroes that players already own will no longer be offered to them when they are prompted to choose a free Hero.
  • Hotkeys: Pressing an Ability hotkey several times in quick succession will no longer cancel targeting mode.
  • Menus: A delicious toaster pastry has been removed from the far-left side of the Alarak Home Screen in ultra-wide resolutions.

We also released a sizable patch last week that brought the Machines of War event, Alarak, many balance adjustments, and more to Heroes of the Storm. If you haven't had a chance to review those patch notes yet, then be sure to click here to get caught up on everything that's recently changed in the Nexus.
