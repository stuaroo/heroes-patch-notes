Heroes Technical Alpha Patch Notes -- Oct 7, 2014

Official Patch Notes v 6.5

A little over a week ago, we brought Heroes of the Storm offline in order to prepare for the final phase of Technical Alpha testing. Today, we’re thrilled to announce that the Calm Before the Storm has passed, and we’ve got a brand new patch available for you. The Heroes Technical Alpha is now back online and you can check out our complete patch notes below.

All Heroes of the Storm Technical Alpha accounts have been reset with today’s patch.

Today’s patch marks our final planned Heroes of the Storm account wipe and monetary refund.

  • All purchases made using real-money prior to today’s account wipe have been reset and refunded to your Battle.net balance.
    • From now on, any real-money and Gold purchases made in the Heroes Shop are permanent and will not need to be reacquired in the future.
  • All in-game Gold earned, as well as any purchases made using Gold, have been wiped clean, along with all experience points, Hero and Player Level progression, Daily Quests, and rewards.
  • Future updates may still require adjustments or wipes for player progression, but there are no further plans to do so, and this will be avoided whenever possible.
  • Please see the Shop section below for more details on purchases using Battle.net Balance.

General

Double XP Week

  • For one week following today’s patch, games played in any matchmaking mode (Practice, Cooperative, and Versus) will award double the normal amount of experience points!

Diablo III: Reaper of Souls Pre-Order Bonus

  • Valla, the Demon Hunter, is now unlocked and playable for all Technical Alpha testers who previously pre-ordered the Reaper of Souls expansion for Diablo III.
  • Please Note: This promotion did not grant Technical Alpha testing access. Players that pre-ordered Reaper of Souls, but have not yet been invited into the Tech Alpha, will receive Valla once they have Heroes of the Storm access in the future.

All Players Have Been Moved to Their Home Battle.net Region

  • Heroes of the Storm now utilizes the friends list that is shared across all other Blizzard games.
  • As mentioned previously, all friend lists built in the previous Technical Alpha phase have been lost.
  • Friends in other Blizzard games can now communicate with Heroes players and see that they are playing Heroes of the Storm.

Support for Additional Regions Coming Soon

  • We’re nearly ready to expand Heroes of the Storm Technical Alpha testing in China, Korea, and Taiwan, and will soon bring servers online in those regions.
  • Once the patch becomes available in China, Korea, and Taiwan, invited players from those regions will be able to make real-money purchases using the Heroes in-game Shop.
  • Additionally, the Heroes of the Storm Technical Alpha game client will soon feature full audio and text translations for the following languages:
    • Korean
    • Simplified Chinese
    • Traditional Chinese

User Interface

Custom Games

  • Early implementation for Custom Games mode has been re-enabled, and can be accessed from the Play screen.
    • Click “Change Mode” on the Play screen, select Custom, and then choose a Battleground to open a Custom Game lobby.
    • Up to 10 player slots, and two observer slots, can be filled by human players. Click the plus icons to invite players to join the Custom Game lobby.
    • A.I. players can be added to fill empty player slots by pressing the Fill A.I. button on the right side of the lobby screen.
    • As the Lobby Host, click the wrench icon below an allied player’s Hero model, or right click an enemy player’s portrait on the right, to access a variety of options for that player.
      • These options include: Swap Team, Kick from Lobby, Set A.I. difficulty, and Promote to Host.
    • The lobby host can click Start Game to begin the match on the selected Battleground once all players have pressed the Ready button.
  • Please note that even though Custom Games have been re-enabled, this feature is not final and work is ongoing. Additional improvements and polish will be added with future game updates.
  • For more details, head over to our recent blog on Custom Games, Observer UI, and Replays.

Observer UI

  • Initial implementation for Observer UI has been added to both Custom Games and Replays.
    • Each team’s selected Heroes, number of Forts remaining, and total Takedowns earned are displayed in the upper-right and left corners of the screen.
    • The in-game timer, Core Health, team levels, and each team’s progress toward the next level are displayed at the top-center of the screen.
  • Please note that this initial iteration of the Observer UI is not final and work is ongoing. Additional improvements and polish will be added with future game updates.
  • For more details, head over to our recent blog on Custom Games, Observer UI, and Replays.

Replay System

  • Initial implementation for the Heroes of the Storm Replay System has been added.
    • Replays can be access by clicking the Watch button, which is now enabled on the menu navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.
    • Select a Replay from the list, and then click Watch Solo in the bottom left corner of the screen to launch the Replay.
    • After launching a Replay, use the controls in the lower left corner of the screen to Pause or Resume (P), speed up (=), or slow down (-) playback. Click anywhere on the Replay Timeline (T) to navigate to that point in the Replay.
  • Once again, please note that this iteration of the Replay System is not final and work is ongoing. Additional improvements and polish will be added with future game updates.
  • For more details, head over to our recent blog on Custom Games, Observer UI, and Replays.

Shop

Please be advised that it is not currently possible to make partial payments when attempting to make real-money purchases using Battle.net Balance.

  • If a player has insufficient funds in his or her Battle.net Balance to make the desired purchase, that player will be required to load additional Balance, or use an alternate payment method.
    • For example: A Heroes player wants to buy a Bundle Pack that costs $20 USD, but only has a Battle.net Balance of $10 USD. That player cannot use those funds toward the final purchase price unless an additional $10 is loaded onto the account (for a total balance of $20,) or an alternate payment method is selected.

Design and Gameplay

    • This is to prevent issues with unintended minimap clicks.

Bug Fixes

General

  • Several typos and tooltip errors have been corrected across various aspects of the game.

Art

  • Black placement indicators should now appear correctly when attempting to place Zagara’s Creep Tumor or Nydus Network with graphics set to Low.

Battlegrounds

  • Attacking a Mercenary Camp at the moment it spawns will no longer cause the defending Mercenaries’ Health to reset mid-fight.
  • The Garden Terror is now properly immune to the effects of an opposing Garden Terror’s Spore Queen’s Curse Ability.

Heroes and Talents

  • Abathur can now properly channel Cursed Hollow Tributes, as well as enter and exit the Haunted Mines after Ultimate Evolution expires.
  • Abathur will now inherit the skin and color variations of allies he copies with Ultimate Evolution
  • Brightwing’s Polymorph no longer occasionally causes the target to become invisible while polymorphed.
  • Chen can now properly enter the Haunted Mines while using his Storm, Earth, Fire Heroic Ability.
  • Chen's Flying Kick will no longer stop working after Stitches’ Hook pulls him to an impassable object.
  • Dealing fatal damage to the Dragon Knight or Garden Terror with Falstad's Stormhammer Talent no longer causes the player that was inside to permanently lose control of their hero.
  • Fixed a rare issue in which stunning Heroes just before they finish channeling certain Heroic Abilities could place the Ability on reduced cooldown without interrupting the cast.
  • E.T.C.'s Guitar Solo Ability will no longer cause a slight delay before his next Basic Attack after learning the Guitar Hero Talent.
  • E.T.C.'s Dance Your Pants Off! Talent can no longer stun Grave Golems.
  • Gazlowe’s Reduce, Reuse, Recycle Talent will no longer cause summoned units to drop Scrap on death.
  • Ghouls summoned by Arthas’ Army of the Dead will no longer instantly die when Arthas is caught by Zagara’s Devouring Maw.
  • Kerrigan can no longer become invulnerable for the duration of her Assimilation Shield buff after landing the killing blow on an enemy Tyrael or Uther.
  • Murky no longer benefits from the Zagara’s Creep Tumor Movement Speed bonus.
  • If Murky’s Egg is destroyed at the exact time he would have respawned, he will now respawn at the Egg’s former location.
  • Bonuses provided by Nova’s Advanced Cloaking Talent are now appropriately lost upon breaking Cloak.
  • Raynor's Bullseye Talent no longer allows Penetrating Round to stun Structures or Grave Golems.
  • Tassadar’s Second Strike Talent no longer allows Psionic Storm to be cast repeatedly for free.
  • Heal over time effects on Tychus are now properly transferred to the Odin upon activating Commandeer Odin.
  • Tyrande's Purging Arrow Talent no longer allows Basic Attacks from Minions, Mercenaries, and Summons to deplete ammo when a Structure is targeted by Hunter’s Mark.

User Interface

  • Hero Portraits in the Player Profile no longer extend beyond the portrait container.
  • Fixed an issue in which players could access locked Skin Variations when queuing for a match with a party.
  • Players can now appropriately swap among the Skins and Mounts they’ve unlocked while searching for a game in the matchmaking queue.
