Heroes of the Storm Balance Patch Notes - October 31, 2018

Official Patch Notes v 2.39.2

We're about to release a new Heroes of the Storm patch to apply a number of balance changes and bug fixes. Read on for details.

Heroes

Assassin Specialist Support Warrior
Falstad Zagara Li Li Artanis
Kel'Thuzad Lucio Blaze
Lunara Tyrande Dehaka

Kel'Thuzad

Abilities

  • Frost Blast (R)
    • Cooldown reduced from 70 to 60 seconds

      • Frost Nova slow increase changed from 10 to 15%

Talents

  • Level 7

Bug Fixes

General

  • Banner: Fixed an issue with the Roll 20 Esports Banner textures.
  • Announcer: Fixed an issue that caused the Mal'Ganis announcer pack to change to Malfurion after loading into a game.

Heroes

  • Ana: Fixed an issue that caused Ana's Level 13 Healing Dart talents to fail to apply to Heroes that were at full health.
  • Mal'Ganis: Fixed an issue that could grant Mal'Ganis vision from summoned units while the Blind as a Bat talent was active.

User Interface

  • Fixed an issue causing Level 5 and Level 15 progression reward icons to appear as white squares.
