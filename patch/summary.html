  9. 2016-09-13

    2

    Patch Notes

    Gameplay Changes

    • Mount movement speed decreased from 40% to 30%
    • Regen globe despawn reduced from 8 to 6 seconds; Mercenary spawn times changed
    • Imposing Presence gets activatable function; Spell Shield now toggleable to control when you'd like to reduce ability damage
    • Relentless talent removed from most Heroes; Bribe talent removed from some Heroes, made Hero-specific for others
    • Map pool reduced to 6 "to increase the frequency with which players encounter the new Battleground, Braxis Holdout"; lasts until October 17

    Play Mode Changes

    • MVP system added: "Five players will receive awards for their in-game performance and individual achievements" at end of each game
    • Ranked changes to promotion and demotion matches; points in excess of division cap are stored until results of promo match, then applied
    • Quest changes for Draft modes: "Any Hero selected...will now count toward progression of any role or universe-specific Daily Quest"
    • Hero League Grandmaster players has been reduced from 500 to 200
    • Try Mode gets Boss camp, can now change enemy hero and/or game speed

    Heroes Tweaked

    • Baseline changes: Johanna gets partial KtP baseline; Muradin gets partial Reverberation baseline
    • Anub'arak, Azmodan, Brightwing, Butcher, Cho, Diablo, E.T.C., Falstad, Greymane, Johanna, Kharazim, Leoric, Li Li, Lt. Morales, Malfurion, Muradin, Murky, Nazeebo, Nova, Raynor, Rehgar, Sgt. Hammer, Sonya, Stitches, Sylvanas, Thrall, Tychus, Uther, Valla, Zagara, Zeratul

    Reworks

  15. 2016-07-12

    Patch Notes

    Gameplay Changes

    • Health sustain changes: regen globes grant more health/mana, longer cooldowns on healing wells
    • Minion Buffs: Lane minions "now deal an additional 50% damage to Structures"
    • Catapults will not acquire a new target after fixating until the target is destroyed or the Catapult is killed
    • Relentless talent more narrowly defined as only stuns, slows, and roots; Executioner bonus damage applied "to anything attacked after the effect is triggered"
    • Healing wells now displayed on mini-map as a '+' icon
    • Garden Terrors now deal more damage to Structures

    Play Mode Changes

    • QM: Bonus XP for auto select when "system detects lengthy queue times for one or more Heroes"
    • Gameplay tips will now display on loading screens when entering a match
    • Rejoin is now optional for Training, Single Player Versus AI, and Custom Games

    Heroes Tweaked

    • Baseline changes: Jaina gets 3 waves of Blizzard baseline; Stitches gets Helping Hand baseline
    • Many changes from devs "doing a pass on all heroes...to increase the health of their talent trees"
    • Abathur, Arthas, Azmodan, Brightwing, Butcher, Chen, Cho, Dehaka, Diablo, E.T.C., Falstad, Gall, Gazlowe, Greymane, Jaina, Kael'thas, Kerrigan, Kharazim, Leoric, Lunara, Medivh, Muradin, Murky, Nazeebo, Nova, Raynor, Sgt.
