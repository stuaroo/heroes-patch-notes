Patch Summary

Returning from a break? Can't recall that one patch that introduced huge gameplay changes? Let Patch Summaries be your guide to recent (and not-so-recent) patch changes.

  1. 2018-06-06

    Balance Update v 2.33.1

    Battleground

    • Structures: outer Tower experience distributed to other Structures "in an effort to keep teams closer in levels for a little while longer"

    Heroes Affected

    • "Baseline Armor has been removed from a number of Heroes": Anub'arak, Arthas, Johanna, Varian

    • Zagara: scaling of Abilities adjusted to "reduce her early-game laning presence...[and] buff her late-game teamfight capability"

    • Anub'arak, Arthas, Auriel, Brightwing, Cassia, Diablo, Fenix, Garrosh, Genji, Johanna, Kael'thas, Lunara, Maiev, Stukov, Thrall, Varian, Zagara
