Patch Summary

Returning from a break? Can't recall that one patch that introduced huge gameplay changes? Let Patch Summaries be your guide to recent (and not-so-recent) patch changes.

  1. 2017-01-06

    Balance Update

    Heroes Affected

    • Dehaka, Diablo, Lt. Morales, Ragnaros, Rehgar, Thrall, Varian, Zul'jin
  2. 2017-01-04

    Patch Notes

    Gameplay

    • Armor system introduced on: Greymane, Arthas, Anub'arak

    Play Mode

    • Tournament Draft added for Custom Games: "allows players to swap their Hero Picks at-will at the end of a draft"

    Battleground

    • Haunted Mines: "Attack Damage scaling for summoned Grave Golems" increased

    Heroes Affected

    • Baseline changes: Azmodan gets movement while channeling All Shall Burn

    • Anub'arak, Arthas, Azmodan, Greymane, Lunara, Nova, Raynor, Sylvanas
  3. 2016-12-20

    Balance Update

    Play Mode

    • TL Party Restrictions Lifted: can now group regardless of rank and with those in placements

    Battleground

    • Haunted Mines: Grave Golem attack damage and stomp damage increased

    Heroes Affected

    • Artanis, Dehaka, Diablo, Ragnaros, Zeratul
  4. 2016-12-14

    3

    Patch Notes

    Play Mode

    • Ranked: HL is now solo queue only; TL expanded to "2, 3, and 5-player parties"

    • TL: will use "First Come, First Serve (FCFS) during Draft Mode" for 2 and 3 player parties; additionally, 2 and 3 player parties must be "ranked within one league of one another"

    • Ranked Leagues: Silver and Gold "will now contain similar amounts of players"; Diamond will now have "about half the number of players than the previous season"

    • Leaver penalties added for Unranked Draft and TL: players with active penalties "can no longer queue for Unranked Draft or Team League games"

    • Brawl: Healing Fountains made Invulnerable for select Brawls (Blackheart's Revenge, Garden Arena, Punisher Arena, Temple Arena)

    UI/Hotkey

    • SHIFT+click a Hero or role "to add a link to that Hero or Role in the chat"; also works by typing Hero or role in square brackets (Ex: [Jaina])

    • Skip button added to MVP screen, and you can now press TAB to "view the in-game Score Screen for that match"

    Battleground

    • Reworked Haunted Mines has returned! Additional mine entrances, new Mercenaries, new layout to underground mines, and Grave Golems "switch lanes each time they are summoned"

    • Warhead Junction: Nuke damage reduced and Nukes now scale at flat rate

    Heroes Affected

    • Artanis, Auriel, Chromie, Dehaka, Diablo, Kael'thas, Nova, Rehgar, Samuro, Zarya

    Reworks
