Heroes of the Storm Balance Update — January 6, 2017

Official Patch Notes v 22.6

We've just released a new patch for Heroes of the Storm in order to apply a few bug fixes and balance changes.

Heroes

Assassin Multi-class Support Warrior
Ragnaros Varian Lt. Morales Dehaka
Thrall Rehgar Diablo

Ragnaros

Abilities

Talents

    • Heroic Difficulty (D)
      • Health and Damage bonus reduced from 50 to 25%

Return

Thrall

Abilities

  • Earthquake (R)
    • Cooldown increased from 60 to 100 seconds
  • ​Sundering (R)
    • Damage increased from 274 to 290

Return

Zul'jin

Abilities

  • Taz'dingo! (R)
    • Cooldown increased from 60 to 90 seconds
    • Mana cost increased from 50 to 75

Developer Comments: We were testing Taz’dingo internally at 60 seconds but had planned on shipping it at 90. This change didn’t make it into the release for Zul’jin but we wanted to get it in place as quickly as possible.

Return

Abilities

  • Charge (E)

    • Mana cost reduced from 50 to 45

Talents

  • Level 4
    • Warbringer (E)
      • Cooldown reduction decreased from 8 to 6 seconds

Return

Support

Lt. Morales

Talents

  • Level 16
    • Inoculation (W)
      • ​Removed

Developer Comments: With the newly implemented Armor system, Inoculation and Shield Sequencer were both doing very similar things for Morales. We like the interaction and decision making that Shield Sequencer grants, so we have decided to keep it and remove Inoculation for now.

Return

Bug Fixes

Heroes, Abilities, and Talents

  • Azmodan: Can no longer mount while channeling All Shall Burn.
  • Dehaka: Fixed an issue that caused Dark Swarm to deal more damage than intended to Minions, and Mercenaries after learning Primal Aggression.
  • Dehaka: Fixed an issue that caused Dark Swarm to deal bonus damage to Monsters after learning Primal Aggression.
