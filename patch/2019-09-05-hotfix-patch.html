Heroes of the Storm Hotfix Patch Notes - September 5, 2019

Official Patch Notes v 2.47.3

We've just released a Heroes of the Storm patch in order to address a few live bugs. Read on for details.

Bug Fixes

Heroes

  • Chen
    • Fixed an issue where Gift of the Ox could cause Chen to take all Stagger damage at once.
    • Purifying Brew no longer reduces crowd control effects on Chen.
  • Rehgar
    • Casting abilities now properly puts Ghost Wolf on cooldown.
  • Valla
    • The Caltrop quest now properly completes at max stacks, and properly grants Vault cooldown and its Mana cost reduction.
