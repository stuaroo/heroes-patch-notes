4 Double Cross Increases the range of Discord Strike by 25% and also hits in a line perpendicular to the forward strike.
4 Extended Lightning Increases Lightning Surge range by 20%.

7 Chaos Reigns Quest: Each time Discord Strike hits a Hero its damage is increased by 3, up to 45. Reward: After hitting 15 Heroes, increase its damage by an additional 45.
7 Endless Energy Quest: Heroes hit between you and the victim permanently decrease the cooldown of Lightning Surge by 0.2 seconds, up to 2 seconds.
7 Thunderstruck Reduces the damage bonus of Sadism by 10%, but Lightning Surge slows enemies hit between you and victim by 70% for 1.5 seconds.
10 Counter-Strike Alarak becomes Protected and channels for 1 second. If an enemy Hero attacked Alarak during that time, Alarak sends a shockwave forward that deals 250 (+4% per level) damage.
10 Deadly Charge After channeling, Alarak charges forward dealing 200 (+4% per level) damage to all enemies in the path. Distance is increased based on the amount of time channeled, up to 1.6 seconds. Issuing a Move order while this is channeling will cancel it at no cost. Taking damage will interrupt the channeling.
13 Dissonance Reduces the damage bonus of Sadism by 10% but increases Discord Strike's silence duration by 0.75 seconds.
13 Hindered Motion Reduces the damage bonus of Sadism by 10% but Telekinesis slows enemies by 40% for 1.5 seconds.
13 Psychokinesis Increases the damage of Telekinesis by 100%.
13 Pure Malice When a nearby Ally Hero dies, your Sadism bonus is increased by 8%, stacking up to 40%. This bonus Sadism cannot be consumed by Hasty Bargain and is lost on death.
16 Negatively Charged Increases damage dealt to enemies hit by the center beam of Lightning Surge by 100%, but does not generate any additional healing.
16 Projected Force Reduces the damage bonus of Sadism by 10% but increases the range of Telekinesis by 25%.
16 Quick Mind Reduces the damage bonus of Sadism by 10% and the cooldown of Telekinesis by 6 seconds.
16 Sustaining Power Increases the healing you receive from dealing damage to Heroes with Lightning Surge by 30%.
20 Counter-Strike Alarak becomes Protected and channels for 1 second. If an enemy Hero attacked Alarak during that time, Alarak sends a shockwave forward that deals 250 damage. This ability will take over your Trait button.
20 Deadly Charge After channeling, Alarak charges forward dealing 200 damage to all enemies in the path. Distance is increased based on the amount of time channeled, up to 1.6 seconds. Issuing a Move order while this is channeling will cancel it at no cost. Taking damage will interrupt the channeling. This ability will take over your Trait button.
20 Hasty Bargain Activate to permanently reduce your Sadism bonus by 4% and reset the cooldowns of your Basic Abilities.
20 Last Laugh Activate to remove all roots, slows, and damage over time effects and teleport to the chosen location. Activating Last Laugh will immediately reduce Alarak's Health to 1.
