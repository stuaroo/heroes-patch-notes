Level Talent Name Description
1 Accelerando Increase the Movement Speed bonus of Wall Ride from 20% to 40% over 4 seconds, and maintain that speed as long as you maintain the Wall Riding movement speed bonus.
1 Maximum Tempo Quest: Kill 5 enemy Heroes while Speed Boost is active. Reward: Increase Amped Speed Boost to 60%.
1 Slip Increase the Movement Speed bonus of Wall Ride to 70% for 1 second when near or passing through an enemy Hero.
1 We Move Together Increase the passive movement speed bonus of Speed Boost to 20% (from 15%).
4 Chase the Bass Quest: After hitting 20 enemy Heroes with Soundwave, increase the arc by 50% and the range by 20%.
4 Off the Wall If you hit an enemy Hero while you have the Wall Riding Movement Speed bonus, reduce the cooldown by 3 seconds.
4 Subwoofer Increase the knockback of Soundwave by up to 50%, based on how close to you they are.
7 Boombox Place a killable ward on the ground that gives nearby allies your Crossfade and Amp It Up auras (auras do not stack). Lasts 30 seconds. Max 1.
7 Loudspeaker If your aura is currently affecting 2 or more allies when you cast Amp It Up, increase its radius by 50% for the duration.
7 Party Mix Quest: Support allies with your aura for a total of 8 minutes (supporting multiple allies rewards more time). Reward: Increase its radius by 20%.
10 Reverse Amp Blast Lucio's Crossfade track at enemy Heroes for 4 seconds, causing Healing Boost to inflict 112 (+4% per level) damage per second and Speed Boost to slow for 45%. Passive: Increase Amp It Up's duration to 4 seconds.
10 Sound Barrier After a 1 second delay, grant yourself and all nearby allied Heroes a 1392 (+4% per level) point shield that rapidly decays over 6 seconds.
13 Back in the Mix Heal for 260 (+4% per level) upon entering a Stun, Silence, or Time Stop effect. This cannot happen more than once every 10 seconds.
13 Beat Mixing After switching Crossfade tracks, if the next song plays for at least 1.5 seconds, Lucio gains a 100 (+4% per level) point Shield that last indefinitely. Switching tacks again removes the Shield.
13 Can't Stop, Won't Stop You cannot be slowed or rooted while you have the Wall Riding Movement Speed bonus.
13 Hard Style Gain 25 Armor while you have the Wall Riding movement speed boost, reducing damage by 25%.
16 Bring it Together If your aura is currently affecting 2 or more allies when you cast Amp It Up, Crossfade's Healing Boost is increased by 40% for the duration.
16 Heliotropics Soundwave also Blinds enemies for 2.5 seconds.
16 Rejuvenescencia While Amp It Up is active, Crossfade's Healing Boost heals for an additional 3% of the target's maximum health per second.
16 Up the Frequency Amp It Up's mana cost is reduced to 80 from 100, and dealing Basic Attack damage to enemy Heroes reduces the cooldown of Amp It Up by 0.35 seconds.
20 Bossa Nova Reduce the cooldown of Sound Barrier to 30 seconds, but the shield now decays over 4 seconds (from 6).
20 Encore Using Crossfade while Amp It Up is active resets the duration of Amp It Up back to its max duration. This can only occur once per cast.
20 Nonstop Remix The duration of Reverse Amp is paused while it effects 2 or more enemy Heroes.
20 Synaesthesia Auditiva Casting Amp It Up removes all Stun, Slow, and Root effects from nearby allies, and all Slow and Root effects on Lucio.
Shortcut Ability Name Description
Q Soundwave Deal 112 (+4% per level) damage to enemies in an area and knock them back.
W Crossfade Play one of two tracks that passively provide allied Heroes with either a Speed Boost or a Healing Boost in a large radius around you. Activate Crossfade to swap tracks. Healing Boost: Restore a small amount of Health every second to Lucio and nearby allied Heroes. Speed Boost: Increase the Movement Speed of Lucio and nearby allied Heroes by 15%.
E Amp It Up Raise Lucio's Crossfade track volume for 3 seconds, amping Healing Boost to 124 (+4% per level) health per second and Speed Boost to 45% increased Movement Speed.
R Sound Barrier After a 1 second delay, grant yourself and all nearby allied Heroes a 1392 (+4% per level) point shield that rapidly decays over 6 seconds.
R Reverse Amp Blast Lucio's Crossfade track at enemy Heroes for 4 seconds, causing Healing Boost to inflict 112 (+4% per level) damage per second and Speed Boost to slow for 45%. Passive: Increase Amp It Up's duration to 4 seconds.
None Wall Ride When moving alongside impassable terrain, Lucio can walk through other units, and his Movement Speed is increased by 20%. This works together with other Movement Speed bonuses.
